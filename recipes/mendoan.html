How To Make Half Fried Mendoan

Description

This is a unique food from Purwokerto that is made from half fermented Tempe(before it's fully becoming tempe)


Ingredients

    • Mendoan as many as you wish
      1/2 tsp stock powder
      5 tbsp wheat flour
      2 tbsp rice flour
      Moderate ice/cold water
      Moderate frying oil
      3 cloves of garlic
      1/2 tsp coriander
      1/2 tsp salt
      1 cm turmeric

    • Steps

    • You can grind the spices using a blender or grind them manually
    • Dissolve the wheat flour and rice flour with ice water, adjust the consistency
    • Add the ground spices to the flour mixture while stirring so that the spices are evenly mixed. Add sliced ​​spring onions
    • Heat the oil. While waiting for the oil to heat, dip the tempeh to be fried into the mixture
    • Fry the mendoan one by one. Don't overcook it too long so it doesn't burn. If you feel it is cooked, remove it then drain it so that the oil is gone
    • Finished. enjoy the food
