How To Make Soy Sauced Chicken

Description

Chicken with the main flavor of sweet(from the soy sauce and palm sugar), sometimes spicy(depend on the regions) and savory


Ingredients

  • Moderate amount of cooking oil
  • 500 grams of chicken
  • 5 green chilies
  • 4 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 lime leaves
  • 1 segment of ginger
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 100 ml of water
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 5 red onions

Steps

  • Fry half cooked chicken, drain the oil.
  • Saute the garlics and onions until fragrant. Then add the crushed lemongrass and ginger.
  • Add bay leaves, lime leaves and green chilies that have been sliced ​​diagonally. Pour in sweet soy sauce and water. Cook until fragrant.
  • Add the chicken, then season with salt.
  • Cook until the chicken is completely cooked and the water has reduced.
  • Finished. enjoy the food
