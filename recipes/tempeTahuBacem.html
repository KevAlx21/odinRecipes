How To Make Half Fried Mendoan

Description

Next is tempe and tofu(Indonesian version of tofu). This food is fermented with soy sauce for that sweetness and savory.


Ingredients

  • 8 large pieces of tofu
  • 8 pieces of tempeh
  • 8 red onion cloves
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp coriander
  • 2 cups tamarind
  • 1 galangal segment
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 1 piece of brown sugar
  • 5 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • Moderate amount of salt

Steps

  • Fry the tofu and tempeh first so that it doesn't fall apart easily when boiled. Just fry briefly and don't dry it too much. Lift & drain.
  • Cook the tofu and tempeh with all the spices except soy sauce. Make sure all ingredients are submerged in water.
  • Cook over medium heat until boiling, then adjust the taste. Add sweet soy sauce, turn the tofu and tempeh so that the spices infuse. Reduce the heat, cook until the water runs out
  • Let it sit for a few moments to cool before frying/serving. If you make it in the afternoon, let it sit overnight so it tastes better then just fry it in the morning.
  • Finished. enjoy the food
